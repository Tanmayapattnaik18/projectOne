What Causes Cancer

Cancer comprises a complex group of diseases with many possible causes. While genetics play a major

role in cancer, other causes of cancer can differ between children and adults.

Childhood Cancers

A mutation or cell change that just happened by chance is

a common cause for cancer in children.

Adult Cancers

In addition to mutations, lifestyle factors like smoking,

tobacco, an unhealthy diet, lack of physical activity, working

with toxic chemicals, or exposure to certain viruses may put

adults at risk for cancers.
